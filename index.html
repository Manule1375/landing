About Me :)

With a Master Degree in both Screenwriting (USA) and Illustration (UK), I have developed an acute eye for visual storytelling with a higher understanding of narrative conceptualising. Through these skills I am able to merge storytelling and art in a unique way that allows the piece (written, or illustrated) to be experienced in multiple dimensions.

Illustration-wise, my strengths lie in Character Design, Coluring and Sequential Art, including stand alone Illustrations.

Writing-wise, I follow emotionally complex narratives and metaphorical realism, using young characters as a perspective point.
